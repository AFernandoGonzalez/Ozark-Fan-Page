Meet "Marty" Byrde. Financial Advisor. Loving Father. Husband. Money Launderer.

When faced with a life or death situation, Marty must decide just how far he is willing to go in order to save his life and the lives of his family. In an attempt to set things right, Marty must use his talents in a race against time to launder $500 Million in 5 years for a Mexican Cartel or seal the fates of his family and himself.

Let the race begin!
