The Usual Suspects

We've all heard the expression, "its always the quiet ones!" Marty fits that idea perfectly. He and his family are unassuming and incredibly average by lifestyle but they are also all very intelligent. Something that becomes more and more of a factor as they use their wits to stay alive. However, they are not the only smart ones in the Ozarks! There is a host of wiley and unsavory characters they must outsmart or partner with in the Ozarks that are equally as intelligent. The Snells and the Langmores for example. They don't take kindly to new neighbors.

Buddy & The Byrdes
Marty Byrde Wendy Byrde Jonah Byrde Charlotte Byrde Buddy Dieker
The overseer of the operation. Marty was the one who started it all in an effort to protect his own life and family. For Marty, it's never more than "do what needs to be done". Marty's wife and a loving mother. Wendy is a political campaign manager by profession. A skill that will serve her well in helping Marty keep their family alive. The son of Marty and Wendy. Jonah is a mix of his parents cunning intelligence and the modern tech savvy of today's youth. A powerfully useful combination! Willful and intelligent, Charlotte has a hard time adjusting to criminal life. She's more concerned with independence and matters of the heart than Mexican Cartels. A tough old man, with a fatal illness that has him waiting to die. Buddy sells his home to the Byrdes under the condition that he comes with it. A blessing in disguise for the Byrdes.

Langmores, Snells & Cartels
Ruth Langmore Wyatt Langmore Jacob Snell Darlene Snell Del
Ruth has the drive, intelligence and quick wit to be anything she desires. Unfortunately, her family's legacy has her moving in the wrong direction. Maybe Marty can help her? The Langmore with the most promise, Wyatt could be the one to change the Langmore curse. That is if he can get out of his own head and away from the family that holds him back. Jacob is a local businessman with deep roots in Ozarks. Power, relationships and reputation are not something he will sacrifice. To Jacob it's all about respect and tradition. The second half of the Snell empire, Darlene is a force to be reckoned with. She's smart, traditional, impulsive and extremely short-tempered. This is both her gift and her curse! The reason it all begins. Del is a Lieutenant in the Mexican Cartel. In a rare moment of graciousness and intrigue, he gives Marty a chance to save the Byrde family.
