Unusually Usual Suspects

We've all heard the expression, "its always the quiet ones!" Marty fits that idea perfectly, Marty and his family are unassuming and incredibly average by lifestyle but they are also all very intelligent. Something that becomes more and more of a factor as the show progresses. Who do you trust? Can you trust those closest to you?
